Industry Background

As a vital sector employing roughly 14% of the U.S. workforce, the healthcare industry is expansive and rapidly evolving on a globally. The integration of information technology has revolutionized healthcare, enhancing efficiency, data accuracy, and security in managing private information.

Healthcare services span across health promotion, disease prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation, encompassing inpatient, diagnostic, emergency, pharmaceutical, and more. The industry's products are broadly categorized into medical equipment, pharmaceutical drugs, and vaccines. With technological advancements, the industry has seen significant improvements in data security and accessibility, reducing manual errors and wait times.

However, this technological reliance comes with increased cybersecurity risks. As healthcare continues to innovate, the protection of sensitive data against cyber threats remains a paramount concern, necessitating robust and intelligent cybersecurity measures.
