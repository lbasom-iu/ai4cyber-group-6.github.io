Visualizations

This section highlights several visualizations that support the analytical approaches used on the NIST NVD and PhishTank datasets.

PhishTank

Healthcare Phishing Incidents by State

Description: This visualization shows a filled map chart of the United States, where color represents the count of healthcare-related PhishTank phishing URLs per state. Red represents the highest count, while blue is lowest. States that are gray were not represented in the healthcare-related subset of Phishtank phishing URLs.

Key Insights: The U.S. has the most phishing attacks related to the healthcare industry. New York has the highest number of incidents.

NIST NVD

Vulnerability Breakdown by Healthcare Keyword

Description: This visualization shows a stacked bar chart with relationships between the top 5 vulnerabilities in the NIST NVD data set and the most common healthcare-related keywords in the descriptions of the data.

Key Insights: The healthcare industry is most targeted by these 5 vulnerabilities (expoit, DoS, cross-site scripting, buffer overflow, and man-in-the-middle). These are the top keywords associated with the top vulnerabilities (medic, health information, surgery, ventilator, pharmacy, PII, imaging, implant, treatment, HIPAA).
